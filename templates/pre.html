Precautions for Blood Cancer

Malignant

✔ Follow a strict medical consultation schedule.

✔ Maintain a balanced diet rich in proteins and vitamins.

✔ Avoid exposure to harmful chemicals and radiation.

✔ Engage in mild physical activities to boost immunity.

Benign

✔ Get regular health checkups to monitor any changes.

✔ Eat a nutrient-rich diet to prevent future complications.

✔ Stay hydrated and avoid excessive processed foods.

✔ Report any unusual symptoms to a healthcare professional.
